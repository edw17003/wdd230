Visual Heirarchy

Apple

https://www.google.com/

Apple is a great example of visual heirarchy. Due to it's top-down presentation of information, it leads the user to keep reading about more of their products.

Hick's Law

Google

https://www.google.com/

The google homepage serves only one primary purpose: to allow the user to make searches. The simplicity of the website shows in their design as well, as the only thing available to do is to make a search.

Whitespace

Glitch

https://glitch.com

There are probably better examples of whitespace available, but Glitch still does a decent job showcasing it. They don't cram information everywhere they can. Instead they use whitespace to keep the user focused on the main message.
